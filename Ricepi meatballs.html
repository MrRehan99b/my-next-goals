Meatballs Ricepi

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground meet (beef,pork,or a mix of boht)
  • 1/2 cup greated parmesan cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt and papper to test
  • Optional:herbs like parsley,basil,or oreganno

    • Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 400F(200C)
    2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until just combined
    3. Use your hands to shape into small balls (about 11/2 inches in diameter)
    4. Place meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
    5. Drizzle with oil and gently roll to coat evenly
    6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through
